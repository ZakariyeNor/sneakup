Right to Cancel (EU 14-Day Rule)

You have the right to cancel your order within 14 days without giving any reason. This aligns with EU consumer protections and Swedish distance contract law.

Conditions for Accepting Returns

Items must be unused, in original packaging, and returned within 14–30 days depending on your region. Items showing wear or damage may be refused or refunded at partial value.

How to Initiate a Return

Email returns@sneakup.example.com with your order number, product details, and reason for return. You will receive step-by-step instructions and a return label if applicable.

Refund Process

Once your return is received and inspected, we’ll notify you via email. Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

Exemptions and Non-returnable Items

We cannot accept returns for custom items, underwear, socks, opened hygiene products, or items marked as final sale. These exclusions comply with EU guidelines.

Return Shipping Costs

Unless the item is incorrect or defective, return shipping costs are the buyer’s responsibility. In some EU jurisdictions, outbound shipping is also refundable—please check local regulations.

Damaged or Defective Products

If you receive a damaged item, contact us within 48 hours of delivery. Include clear photos and packaging evidence. We will arrange for a replacement or refund.

Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund within 10 business days, first check with your bank or credit card provider. If the issue persists, contact us at returns@sneakup.example.com.
